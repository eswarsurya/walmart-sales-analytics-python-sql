# Report Summary

This project explores Walmart weekly sales data to understand store performance, annual sales trends, holiday-week behaviour, and possible external factors such as fuel price, CPI, temperature, and unemployment.

## Analysis Focus

The work focuses on retail sales performance and business reporting: which stores generate the most sales, how sales change over time, whether holiday weeks differ from non-holiday weeks, and how economic/weather fields relate to weekly sales.

## Business Value

The project shows how Python and SQL-style analysis can turn retail data into a clear business review. The outputs are designed for quick scanning by analysts, hiring managers, or recruiters reviewing practical analytics work.
